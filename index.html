Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about MSPL Yoga Certification and YCB.

MSPL Yoga Certification is a professional certification program that validates the skills and knowledge of yoga practitioners. It ensures that certified individuals meet industry standards for teaching and practicing yoga safely and effectively.

YCB (Yoga Certification Board) is the governing body that sets standards for yoga education and certification. It's important because it ensures uniformity, quality, and recognition of yoga certifications both nationally and internationally.

Anyone who has completed the required yoga training hours from a recognized institute can apply for the MSPL Yoga Certification exam. This includes yoga teachers, practitioners, and fitness professionals seeking formal certification.

You can register for the YCB Yoga exam through MSPL by visiting our official website, filling out the registration form, submitting the required documents, and paying the examination fee online through our secure payment gateway.
